About Oman

Oman is a country located on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, bordered by the United Arab Emirates to the northwest, Saudi Arabia to the west, and Yemen to the south. With a rich cultural heritage, Oman is known for its stunning landscapes, from vast deserts to lush mountains and beautiful coastlines.

The capital city, Muscat, is a thriving port city with a blend of traditional and modern influences. Oman is famous for its friendly people, diverse culture, and historical significance. The country has a long maritime history, which has helped shape its trade routes and relationships with many civilizations.
